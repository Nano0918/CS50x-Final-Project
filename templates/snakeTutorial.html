Snake Tutorial

  • Press Start to begin the game
  • Press either (W,A,S,D) or your arrow keys to move the snake
  • Try to eat the food found around the map
  • The more food you eat the bigger you grow
  • As the snake you cannot intersect your own body
  • Good luck and try to see how big you can get
